Programming is the process of creating instructions that enable computers to perform tasks and solve problems. It plays a crucial role in various fields such as software development, data analysis, artificial intelligence, and web development. The benefits of programming are vast; it enhances efficiency and productivity by automating repetitive tasks, enables innovation through the development of new technologies, and improves problem-solving skills. Furthermore, programming drives economic growth by creating job opportunities and empowering businesses to operate more effectively in a digital world.
